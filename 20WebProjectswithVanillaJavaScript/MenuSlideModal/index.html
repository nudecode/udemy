Tell Me More

Vanessa Allen is a registered psychologist, a member of the Australian Psychological Society (APS), a STAP trained Board Approved Supervisor. and a previous committee member of the APS Executive Committee, Brisbane and North Queensland Area.

Vanessa has experience working with children, adults and families in administering psychometric assessments, counselling and psycho educational groups; long term unemployed and people with disabilities in their return to work; veterans and their families, case managing external providers; cancer care, with individuals couples and families; employee assistance providers services; crisis and trauma care; and medico-legal and work cover clients in private practice.

Vanessa’s areas of interest include the supervision, mentoring and developing of psychologists, and improving the health and wellbeing of people with mental illness and adjustment difficulties, particularly those who have experienced trauma; by providing evidence based, clinical interventions and support.
